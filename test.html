Nature vs. Nurture

Before diving into specific theories, we must address the fundamental debate in developmental psychology: Nature (Biology/Genetics) versus Nurture (Environment/Experience).

Key Concept: Continuous vs. Discontinuous development. Does change happen gradually, like a tree growing, or in distinct stages, like a ladybug's life cycle?

Erikson's Psychosocial Stages

Erik Erikson proposed that we pass through eight stages of development, each defined by a specific conflict.

Age Conflict Virtue Gained
0–1.5 Years Trust vs. Mistrust Hope
1.5–3 Years Autonomy vs. Shame/Doubt Will
3–6 Years Initiative vs. Guilt Purpose
6–12 Years Industry vs. Inferiority Competence
